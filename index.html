Our Legacy of Innovation

From Rebounces to Central Tennis

Our roots trace back to Rebounces, where we invented the Green Tennis Machine—revolutionizing tennis ball pressurizing technology. We partnered with Laykold to recycle the first tennis balls into the court surfacing.

Today, as Central Tennis and a Laykold Authorized Masters Dealer, we continue that tradition of innovation and excellence.

Serving facilities across six states, we've completed projects for:

  • Universities and collegiate athletic facilities
  • Municipal parks and recreation departments
  • Private country clubs and racquet clubs
  • Tennis centers and academies

Our expertise in manufacturer specifications, proper curing times, and technical requirements ensures every project meets the highest standards—protecting your investment and delivering surfaces built to last.
